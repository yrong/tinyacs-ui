Google Font - Open Sans

Usage:
In your .less file anywhere in your project, just add import for your font. E.g. to use Opensans Light Italic, `@import "/packages/google-opensans-less/less/opensans-300italic.import.less";`

Source:
Fonts are downloaded @ http://www.google.com/fonts/#UsePlace:use/Collection:Open+Sans
